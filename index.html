About Tutorlyra

Tutorlyra is a student-run online tutoring platform where peers help each other learn and succeed. Flexible, safe, and focused on school subjects.

How It Works

  1. Browse tutors on the Tutors page
  2. Book a session at least 24 hours in advance
  3. Attend your session online via Zoom or Google Meet
  4. Provide feedback to improve the platform

Rules & Safety

  • All sessions are online only
  • Bookings must be made 24+ hours in advance
  • No personal contact info shared
  • School-appropriate subjects only
  • Respect your tutor’s time
