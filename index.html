Welcome to Tutorlyra

Tutorlyra connects students with peer tutors from our school. Whether you need help in Math, Science, English, or other subjects, we make learning flexible, safe, and fun. All sessions are online and must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.

About Tutorlyra

Tutorlyra is run by students, for students. Our goal is to create a safe and effective peer tutoring network that helps everyone succeed academically. Tutors are selected based on subject expertise, reliability, and experience.

Why choose Tutorlyra?

  • Flexible scheduling to fit your school life
  • Verified student tutors from our school
  • Online sessions for convenience and safety
  • Focus on school subjects and academic support
  • Friendly, supportive, and patient tutors

How It Works

  1. Browse our tutors and their subjects.
  2. Book a session at least 24 hours in advance.
  3. Receive the Zoom or Google Meet link after booking.
  4. Attend the session online and get personalized help.
  5. Provide feedback to help improve the tutoring experience.

Rules & Safety

  • All sessions are online only.
  • Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance.
  • No last names or personal contact info are shared publicly.
  • Only school-appropriate subjects are allowed.
  • Respect your tutor’s time and effort.
  • Sessions are confidential and safe.
