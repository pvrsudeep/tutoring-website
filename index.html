Welcome to Tutorlyra

Tutorlyra is a student-run platform that connects peers at our school with talented tutors. We make learning easier, flexible, and fun. All sessions are online only and must be booked at least 24 hours in advance.

About Us

Tutorlyra was founded by students who wanted to create a safe and effective tutoring network for everyone. Our tutors are carefully selected based on subject expertise, experience, and reliability. Whether you need help with Math, Science, English, or Coding, Tutorlyra has a peer tutor for you.

Why choose Tutorlyra?

  • Flexible scheduling to fit your school life
  • All tutors are verified students from our school
  • Online sessions for convenience and safety
  • Focus on school subjects and real academic support
  • Friendly and supportive environment

Check out our full list of tutors to find the perfect match for your learning needs.

Rules & Safety

  • All sessions are online only (Zoom or Google Meet)
  • Bookings must be made at least 24 hours in advance
  • No last names or personal contact info listed publicly
  • Only school-appropriate subjects are allowed
  • Respect your tutor’s time and effort
  • Sessions are confidential and safe
