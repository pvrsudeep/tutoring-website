About Tutorlyra

Our Mission

Tutorlyra was created by students for students. We provide a safe, online platform where peers can support each other academically, making learning flexible and engaging.

Why Choose Us?

  • Flexible scheduling to fit school life
  • Verified student tutors
  • Online sessions for convenience and safety
  • Focus on school subjects and academic growth
  • Friendly and supportive environment

Subjects We Cover

  • Math: Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Calculus
  • Science: Biology, Chemistry, Physics
  • English: Writing, Literature, Public Speaking
  • Social Studies: History, Civics
  • Computer Science & Coding

How It Works

Step 1: Browse Tutors

Check out our tutors, their subjects, and availability.

Step 2: Book a Session

Book at least 24 hours in advance to secure your spot.

Step 3: Attend Online

Receive your Zoom/Google Meet link and join the session online.

Step 4: Feedback

Give feedback to improve the tutoring experience for everyone.

Rules & Safety

  • All sessions are online only.
  • Bookings must be made 24+ hours in advance.
  • No last names or personal contact info shared.
  • Only school-appropriate subjects allowed.
  • Respect your tutor’s time and effort.
  • Sessions are confidential and safe.

We prioritize safety and learning. Tutors are verified students from our school, and all sessions follow school-appropriate standards.
